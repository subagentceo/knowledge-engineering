# How delegation shows up in practice

- **Course design (educators):** decide what work is needed — content selection, sequencing, learning objectives — then decide who does what (human / AI / both). Subject-matter and pedagogical expertise drive the split.
- **Disciplinary analysis:** *Reveal problem anatomy* (break a challenge into component parts), *map AI possibilities* (what can be automated, augmented, or handed to AI agents), and *design decision trees* (frameworks for when and how to involve AI, built from real case studies).
- **Career/learning (students):** AI gathers information and coaches; the human drives strategy, decisions, and values.

# Delegation

> Distilled from the *AI Fluency for Educators / Students* and *Teaching AI Fluency* courses.

## Overview

**Delegation = determining what work should be done by humans, by AI, or together.** Answer **What** and **Why** before you decide **Who** and **How**.

- [Three types of delegation awareness](references/awareness-types.md) — Problem Awareness, Platform Awareness, Task Delegation (human vs AI strengths).
- [How delegation shows up in practice](references/delegation-in-practice.md) — course design, disciplinary analysis, career/learning.
- [The Delegation–Diligence loop](references/delegation-diligence-loop.md) — the strategic half of the strategic/ethical loop.

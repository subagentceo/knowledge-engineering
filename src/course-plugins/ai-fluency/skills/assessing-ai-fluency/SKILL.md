# Assessing AI Fluency

> Distilled from the *Teaching AI Fluency* course.

## Overview

Assess AI fluency by combining outcome, process, and reflection evidence across the 4Ds — never output alone — focusing on observable actions and concrete artifacts while keeping grading load manageable.

- [Three combined assessment approaches](references/assessment-approaches.md) — outcome-, process-, and reflection-based, and the guiding principle.
- [Assessing each D](references/assessing-each-d.md) — outcome/process/reflection evidence for Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence.
- [The four assessment matrices](references/assessment-matrices.md) — per-D matrices and how to turn them into rubrics.
- [Key artifacts and managing volume](references/artifacts-and-volume.md) — what to collect and how to keep grading load manageable.

# Three combined assessment approaches

Assess AI fluency by combining all three — don't rely on output alone:

- **Outcome-based** — what students *produce* through AI collaboration; did they achieve the stated goals via partnership?
- **Process-based** — *how* they work with AI over time: iteration patterns, recovery from failure, methodological sophistication.
- **Reflection-based** — metacognitive awareness: *why* strategies worked/didn't, what was learned, future applications.

Guiding principle: focus on **observable actions and concrete artifacts**, not assumptions about understanding. Make invisible decision-making visible through documentation and reflection. Assessment should be a **learning opportunity, not just measurement** — students should know you care about *how* they learn to work with AI, not only what they produce.

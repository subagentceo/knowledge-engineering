# Key artifacts

Chat logs (primary process record) · diligence statements (transparency/attribution) · learning journals (reflection) · decision logs (kept/modified/discarded) · process playbooks (personal strategy).

# Managing assignment volume

- **Detailed deliverable-based rubrics** — clear, granular deliverables for fast verification.
- **Self- and peer-review emphasis** — students, with guidance, are well placed to observe development.
- **Lightning-round conferences** — short (~5-min) discussions in place of written feedback.
- **Selective sampling** — don't read every word; have students flag key moments.

# Diligence

> Distilled from the *AI Fluency for Educators / Students* and *Teaching AI Fluency* courses.

## Overview

**Diligence = taking responsibility for AI use** through ethical, transparent, and accountable practice. Don't outsource decisions: you remain accountable for what you produce and, for educators, for what reaches students.

- [Three types of diligence](references/diligence-types.md) — Creation, Transparency, and Deployment diligence.
- [Diligence for educators](references/diligence-for-educators.md) — owning what reaches students; modeling responsible use.
- [Diligence as field-values embodiment](references/field-values.md) — for disciplines: codify ethics, clarify transparency norms, co-create accountability.
- [The Delegation–Diligence loop](references/delegation-diligence-loop.md) — the ethical half of the strategic/ethical loop.

# Three types of diligence

- **Creation Diligence** — thoughtfully *select* the AI system: consider privacy, security, and appropriateness for the task. For students: follow your school's AI policies and use approved systems.
- **Transparency Diligence** — *honestly disclose* AI collaboration to professors, teammates, employers, or students. Develop disclosure skills; document AI contributions.
- **Deployment Diligence** — *take ownership* of AI-assisted outputs: verify accuracy, check appropriateness, and be able to **explain every part** of what you submit and apply the concepts to new situations.

# The two nested loops (from Teaching AI Fluency)

The 4Ds organize into two loops that constantly inform each other:

- **Delegation–Diligence loop** = the *strategic and ethical* container. Sets direction and boundaries. Forward (Delegation → Diligence): strategic decisions raise ethical questions. Reverse (Diligence → Delegation): ethical constraints clarify and improve strategic choices.
- **Description–Discernment loop** = the *tactical, moment-to-moment craft* of building a shared **cognitive environment** (shared vocabulary, goals, methods, interaction patterns) through conversations, not single prompts. You describe a need, evaluate the result, refine the description — iteratively.

Nested: the Delegation–Diligence loop is the strategic container; the Description–Discernment loop fills it with rich interaction. The result is responsible, sophisticated collaboration that transcends what either partner achieves alone.

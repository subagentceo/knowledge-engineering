# The load-bearing distinction: augmentation vs automation

- **Augmentation** = AI helps *you* do your work better. Preferred, especially in learning.
- **Automation** = AI does the work *for* you without deep thought. Problematic for learning ("borrowed intelligence" weakens critical thinking).

The framing across all three courses: AI **enhances** human capability, it does not replace human insight, judgment, or care. The goal for students is to be **irreplaceable, not replaced**.

# The 4D Framework Overview

> Distilled from the *AI Fluency for Educators / Students* and *Teaching AI Fluency* courses.

## Overview

AI Fluency is using AI **effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely** through *enduring principles* rather than temporary tips, tools, or prompts. It is captured by four interconnected competencies — the **4Ds** — that work together and stay relevant as the technology evolves. This skill orients you before going deeper into any single D.

- [The four Ds](references/the-four-ds.md) — the four competencies and the three sub-competencies inside each.
- [Augmentation vs automation](references/augmentation-vs-automation.md) — the load-bearing distinction; irreplaceable, not replaced.
- [Human in the loop](references/human-in-the-loop.md) — keeping decision-making control; the 4Ds as its operational form.
- [The two nested loops](references/two-nested-loops.md) — the Delegation–Diligence and Description–Discernment loops.
- [Teaching approaches](references/teaching-approaches.md) — the four ways to teach the framework and the reusable context document.

# Being the human in the loop & a personal AI policy

You keep decision-making control: decide what to ask, evaluate outputs, decide if they're usable. Write a **personal AI collaboration policy** with sections for: usage boundaries (incl. "red lines" where you won't use AI), learning applications (AI as tutor, not completer), transparency standards, skill maintenance (regular AI-free intervals; watch for over- *and* under-reliance), ethical guidelines, and continuous growth. Keep it realistic, concise, dated, and revisit it.

Reflection questions: Is this engagement helping me grow? Am I building skills for my future or just completing tasks? Am I honest about AI's role? Does this align with my values?

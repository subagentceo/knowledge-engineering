# AI Fluency for Students

> Distilled from the *AI Fluency for Students* course.

## Overview

The whole student angle rests on one distinction: **using AI to do your work** (borrowed intelligence — weakens critical thinking) vs **using AI as a learning partner** (builds knowledge and capability). Over-reliance shows up later as: failing exams without AI, struggling to explain your thinking in interviews, inability to judge AI advice, and real-world problem-solving gaps.

- [AI as a learning partner](references/learning-partner.md) — the 4Ds applied to learning, plus the living learning journal.
- [AI in career planning](references/career-planning.md) — information-gathering and coaching vs human strategy; authenticity in the job search.
- [Being the human in the loop & a personal AI policy](references/human-in-the-loop-policy.md) — decision control, the personal AI collaboration policy, and reflection questions.

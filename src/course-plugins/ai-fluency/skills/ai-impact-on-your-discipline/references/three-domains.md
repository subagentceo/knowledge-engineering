# Analyze impact across three domains

- **Curriculum** — identify what AI can automate; decide which foundational concepts grow more/less important; map how AI changes best practices; develop compelling discipline-specific answers to "why learn X when AI does it?"; define how to direct AI agents.
- **Pedagogy** — enhancement (personalized tutoring at scale, interactive simulations, immediate feedback) vs risk (distinguishing AI that *enhances* learning from AI that *shortcuts* it). Address the reality that students will use AI regardless.
- **Assessment** — the most urgent disruption: when students generate content in seconds, *what are we assessing?* Identify authentic demonstrations of AI-assisted learning, create shortcut-proof assignments, value process/growth over products, and assess both AI-assisted and human-only work.

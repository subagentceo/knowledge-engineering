# Three key questions for graduates

1. **What gets automated?** — which routine career tasks AI will handle.
2. **Partnership potential** — where human–AI collaboration is most impactful.
3. **Who's in charge?** — how humans manage and stay accountable for AI work.

## Disruption is uneven

Disruption is **not uniform** across disciplines: curriculum may stay stable while assessment transforms; pedagogy may evolve while core content holds. Some disruptions are opportunities to leverage, others are problems to solve — **your expertise determines which is which.**

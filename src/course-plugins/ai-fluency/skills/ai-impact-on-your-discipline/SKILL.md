# AI's Impact on Your Discipline

> Distilled from the *AI Fluency for Educators* and *Teaching AI Fluency* courses.

## Overview

**Core principle:** AI fluency amplifies human expertise, it does not replace it. Disciplinary knowledge is the foundation for unprecedented achievement through AI partnership. The goal is to prepare students to be **irreplaceable, not replaced**.

- [Three key questions for graduates](references/key-questions.md) — what gets automated, partnership potential, who's in charge; why disruption is uneven.
- [Three types of expertise you bring](references/three-expertises.md) — disciplinary, pedagogical, assessment; and the irreplaceable human capabilities.
- [Analyze impact across three domains](references/three-domains.md) — curriculum, pedagogy, assessment.
- [Apply the 4Ds to disciplinary expertise](references/4ds-for-disciplines.md) — Discernment, Description, Delegation, Diligence for a field, and the feedback-loop payoff.

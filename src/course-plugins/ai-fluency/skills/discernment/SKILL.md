# Discernment

> Distilled from the *AI Fluency for Educators / Students* and *Teaching AI Fluency* courses.

## Overview

**Discernment = recognizing good vs bad results and adapting accordingly** through critical evaluation. You don't just accept or reject AI output — you explain *why* it works or doesn't, in context, and use that to shape better future output.

- [Three levels of discernment](references/levels.md) — Product, Process, and Performance discernment.
- [Discernment as quality evaluation](references/quality-evaluation.md) — for educators and disciplines: build criteria, collect exemplars, diagnose failures.
- [Discernment as a learning check](references/learning-check.md) — for students: comprehension tests and information verification.
- [The Description–Discernment loop](references/description-discernment-loop.md) — the continuous feedback loop with Description.

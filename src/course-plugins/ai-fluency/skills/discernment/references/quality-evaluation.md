# Discernment as quality evaluation (educators / disciplines)

This is where domain expertise earns its keep:

- **Build quality criteria** — articulate excellence beyond vague terms; write detailed rubrics capturing deep quality markers students can internalize and apply.
- **Collect outstanding work** — analyze exemplars systematically *with* students; make expert thinking visible through annotation guides.
- **Diagnose failures** — examine flawed examples forensically; understand failure modes (for working alone and with AI).

For course design, evaluate AI suggestions against your own expertise: "Does this concept actually help *these* target students?" Recognize unexpected insights too, not only errors.

# Three levels of discernment

- **Product Discernment** — evaluate the AI's *creations*: accuracy, helpfulness, and whether they offer genuinely new/useful perspectives.
- **Process Discernment** — examine the AI's *reasoning*: its assumptions and logical steps. Does the path make sense, not just the answer?
- **Performance Discernment** — assess whether the AI *behaved helpfully in the role* you assigned (did the "critical editor" actually critique?).

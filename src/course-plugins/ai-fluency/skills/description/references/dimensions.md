# Three dimensions of description

- **Product Description** — specify the *final result*: length, key messages/content, audience, style, format. What you want the AI to produce.
- **Process Description** — guide the AI's *approach*: "think step-by-step," "consider multiple perspectives," walk through your reasoning, use a particular method.
- **Performance Description** — define the AI's *behavior/role during the interaction*: a critical editor, a supportive brainstormer, a Socratic tutor, a debate partner.

# ai-fluency

A Claude Code plugin that packages the **AI Fluency** framework into trigger-on-demand skills for educators and students. The framework's core is the **4Ds — Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence** — four interconnected competencies for using AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely while staying the responsible human in the loop (augmentation over automation).

## What it covers

- The 4D framework as a whole, then one skill per D with its three sub-competencies and the two loops (Delegation–Diligence, Description–Discernment).
- The educator workflow: applying the 4Ds to course design and learning outcomes, and to learning materials and assignments.
- Designing AI-fluent assignments from the component guide (outcome / process / reflection-based).
- Assessing AI fluency (outcome / process / reflection-based assessment and the four assessment matrices).
- Analyzing AI's impact on a specific discipline across curriculum, pedagogy, and assessment.
- The student-facing angle: AI as a learning partner, career planning, and being the human in the loop.

## Source courses

All skills are distilled only from these three transcripts:

- *AI Fluency for Educators* — `projects/courses/ai-fluency-for-educators__aif4ed.txt`
- *AI Fluency for Students* — `projects/courses/ai-fluency-for-students__aif4students.txt`
- *Teaching AI Fluency* — `projects/courses/teaching-ai-fluency__taif.txt`

## Who it's for

**Educators** designing courses, building materials, writing assignments, assessing student work, and leading institutional AI conversations — and **students** learning to use AI to build (not bypass) their own understanding and career.

## Skills

| Skill | One-liner |
|---|---|
| `the-4ds-overview` | The whole framework: the 4Ds, augmentation vs automation, human in the loop, the two nested loops, and the four teaching approaches. |
| `delegation` | Deciding what work goes to human, AI, or both: Problem / Platform / Task awareness. |
| `description` | Communicating with AI as conversation, not commands: Product / Process / Performance description. |
| `discernment` | Critically evaluating AI work and adapting: Product / Process / Performance discernment and the Description–Discernment loop. |
| `diligence` | Responsible, transparent, accountable AI use: Creation / Transparency / Deployment diligence. |
| `designing-ai-fluent-assignments` | Building assignments with the component guide and applying the 4Ds to course design, materials, and learning outcomes. |
| `assessing-ai-fluency` | Outcome / process / reflection-based assessment, key artifacts, the four matrices, and managing assignment volume. |
| `ai-impact-on-your-discipline` | Mapping AI's effect on a field across curriculum / pedagogy / assessment and applying the 4Ds to disciplinary expertise. |
| `ai-fluency-for-students` | The student angle: AI as learning partner, career planning, and a personal AI collaboration policy. |

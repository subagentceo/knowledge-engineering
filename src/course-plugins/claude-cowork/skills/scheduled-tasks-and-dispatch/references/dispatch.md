# Dispatch — Start a Task from Your Phone

Dispatch covers tasks you start while away from your computer entirely. Assign the task from the **Claude mobile app**; the work runs on your desktop — **same files, same connectors, same permissions**; you get a push notification when it's done.

**Constraint (same as scheduled tasks):** Dispatch only works when your desktop is on, awake, and signed in with Cowork open. If your machine is asleep or off, Claude holds the task until you're back.

**Availability:** Dispatch is in research preview, currently on Pro and Max plans. On Enterprise it may not be available, and where it is, your IT admin sets it up — so if you don't see Dispatch, that's likely why.

## Safety note

Scheduled tasks run while you're not watching — until you're confident a task runs the way you need, prompt it to **draft for your review** rather than send on your behalf, and let the first run go against a copy of the data.

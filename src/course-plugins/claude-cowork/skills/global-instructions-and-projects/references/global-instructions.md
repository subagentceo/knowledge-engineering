# Global Instructions — The Brief That Applies to Every Session

A standing brief you write once in **Settings → Cowork → Global instructions → Edit**. Claude references it during every session — every chat, scheduled task, and Dispatch.

What to put in it:
- **Who you are and what you do.**
- **Shorthand and acronyms you use** (so Claude doesn't ask what "the QBR deck" is).
- **How you like output delivered** — format, length, tone.

It doesn't need to be complete on day one. As you work, corrections you keep giving (*"lead with the bottom line," "don't use Oxford commas"*) are global-instruction candidates. *Example shape:* "I'm a [role] at [company], working on [streams]. Common acronyms: […]. Most deliverables are [docs/decks/briefs]. Keep updates concise — lead with the recommendation. I review the .md previews before creating the final file."
